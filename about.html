Who We Are

Sibrew Technologies provides practical technology solutions focused on reliability, simplicity, and real-world use.

The work centres around designing and building software and hardware systems that solve everyday operational problems for small organisations, community groups, makerspaces, and individuals.

What We Do

  • Custom software applications
  • Database-driven systems
  • Automation and workflow tools
  • Embedded and kiosk systems
  • Hardware / software integration
  • Small-scale infrastructure and deployments

Our Approach

Solutions are designed to be understandable, maintainable, and robust. Preference is given to open standards, open-source tooling, and systems that can operate locally without relying entirely on cloud services.

Build small. Build robust. Build for longevity.
